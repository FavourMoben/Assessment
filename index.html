What is New Orleans famous for?

New Orleans is renowned for its extravagant Mardi Gras celebration, jazz clubs, 18th-century buildings, and thriving practice of voodoo. The annual festivity often lasts for 2 weeks, with loud music, quirky costumes, and elaborate floats parading through the streets of the city.
